About Us

Kwynch aims towards answering mind boggling questions which have either remained unanswered or questions of which the general public is unaware. We at Kwynch are thriving to make this information easier to access and understand. We strive to organise the most sought after topics in this vast universe and present it to our readers in the form of simple & unambiguous articles aided with pictorial representation which are written and designed by people who have considerable insight into the respective topics.

All we need is, consolidate your thoughts and help us write accurate content on any of the fields you have specialised in. In return for your generosity, we promise to give you complete credits for your selfless help. Come forward and join hands with us to share your acquired knowledge on any expertise be it any field. Let your knowledge not just help us grow but also help the world Kwynch their thirst of knowledge.

FAQs

How Many hours/posts we need from you?

Well this can vary from person to person according to their ability. If you are creating a completely original content we need at least 3 articles every week from you. If you are copying the material from some other site we need at least 5 articles every week. This will be 7-8 hours per week.

What is the word limit for article?

At Kwynch we believe that a picture speaks more than words. Our every article will contain pictures so we need an interesting article from your side with a word range of just 200-300 words.

What we have for you in return?

For your generosity, we will give you complete credits by mentioning your name with every post of yours at every platform where we mention you article. Also a well written letter of appreciation from our side will surely add up to your resume. Not just this if you continue working with us really well we might end up paying you a good amount for each article from your side.

Is it required that you visit us?

No, you are not required to visit us. Our team will be in touch with you over electronic media.

Can I take breaks if I have other engagements?

Yes, if you need a break just inform us and enjoy your break. You just need to tell us for what duration you will not be there with us.

Will you have the copyright over your work piece?

No. we will give you complete credits for your work at every platform but your work will be a property of Kwynch. So if unfortunately you are not able to work with us more, even then we will have complete rights over your previous work piece.
